Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

We value your privacy. This policy explains how BELINA CAR collects, uses, and protects personal information for our website and the upcoming mobile app.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you share when booking (name, phone, email).
  • Trip information (pickup date/time, duration, selected car).
  • Basic device or browser data for security and analytics.

How We Use Information

  • To process and confirm your bookings.
  • To communicate with you about your reservation or support requests.
  • To improve our services, site reliability, and customer experience.

Legal Basis

We process your data to perform a contract (your booking), with your consent, or for our legitimate interests (service improvement and security).

Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide services and to comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted providers (e.g., payment, messaging, hosting) strictly to deliver our services.

Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, as permitted by law.

Contact

Questions or requests: booking@belina.car
